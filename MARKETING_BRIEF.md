## 2. One-line pitch

Poker Logic Lab is a poker decision-training app that teaches you to read opponents and make math-correct calls, folds, and raises, scored on the quality of your decisions instead of whether you got lucky.

## 3. Short pitch (for ads, bios, app stores)

Most players lose because they guess. Poker Logic Lab turns guessing into knowing. You practice real hands, read your opponent's likely cards, and get scored on whether your decision was profitable long-term, not on this one result. It teaches the math (pot odds, equity, EV, ICM) as a tool to win a read, not as dry drills. Pay once, keep it forever.

## 4. Long pitch (for landing pages, sales emails)

Every poker player has felt it: you made what seemed like a fine call, lost the pot, and had no idea if you were wrong or just unlucky. That uncertainty is what keeps players stuck.

Poker Logic Lab fixes it by training the thing that actually separates winners from everyone else: making good decisions under uncertainty. You replay real hands one street at a time. At each decision it pauses and asks what you'd do, you read the range of hands your opponent could be holding, and you choose to call, fold, or raise. Then it shows you the correct play and, more importantly, the plain-English reason why.

The scoring is the magic. Instead of chip counts that reward luck, you earn points for +EV decisions. Make the right call and lose the pot? Your score still goes up. That single reframe, from results to decisions, is what changes how a player thinks.

Under the hood is a full poker math engine (equity, pot odds, expected value, outs, ICM, ranges) that runs instantly in your browser. But you never have to be a math person. It teaches the numbers as you go, in a plain-language guide built for people starting from zero.

One-time price. No subscription. No expensive solver. Just the fastest way to stop leaking money and start making confident, profitable decisions.

## 5. Who it's for (audience + personas)

- **The frustrated recreational player.** Plays cash games weekly, breaks even or loses slowly, suspects they have leaks but can't find them. Wants to plug leaks without hiring a coach.
- **The aspiring tournament player.** Cashes sometimes, bricks the bubble often. Needs ICM and push/fold math they can actually use.
- **The home-game grinder.** Plays with the same crew every week. Wants to exploit specific opponents (the calling station, the nit, the maniac).

- **The solver refugee.** Tried GTO Wizard or PokerSnowie, found them expensive and abstract, and wants something that builds intuition instead of memorization.

**Emotional drivers:** the sting of not knowing if you played a hand right, the frustration of losing to worse players, the desire to feel in control at the table, and hatred of monthly subscriptions.

## 6. The tools (features + the benefit of each)

1. **Hand Replay** (the core loop) — Real hands play out and pause at every decision. You read the opponent's range and choose call/fold/raise, then get the correct play plus the reason. *Benefit: learn to make the right decision in real spots, and understand why.*
2. **Equity Visualizer** — All 169 starting hands on a grid colored by strength. Drag an opponent's range and watch your win odds update live. *Benefit: finally see which hands are actually strong and how that changes vs different opponents.*
3. **Mental Math Blitz** — 30-second rapid-fire quizzes on pot odds and EV. *Benefit: do the key math fast enough to use it live, no calculator.*
4. **Equity Calculator** — Your hand vs any range on any board, with the "is the price right" answer. *Benefit: settle any spot in seconds.*
5. **Tournament Lab** — Push/fold and ICM training. *Benefit: stop torching your tournament life near the money.*
6. **Adversary Lab** — Model a real opponent from six simple behavioral sliders, then train against their exact style. *Benefit: build your strategy around the actual people you beat up on (or lose to).*
7. **EV Dashboard** — Tracks your decision quality over time, auto-detects your leaks, and serves targeted drills. *Benefit: a personal coach that finds your weak spots and fixes them.*

- **It teaches reads, not just charts.** Competitors teach the math as the destination. Poker Logic Lab makes the math a tool for reading a real opponent's range, which is the actual skill gap.

- **Adversary profiling.** Build the math around specific people you play against. No competitor does this.
- **Automatic leak detection + drills.** It finds your recurring mistakes and drills them with spaced repetition.
- **Instant, no lag.** The math runs in your browser, so range sliders and equity update in real time.
- **One-time price, no subscription.** The poker community openly resents subscription training tools. $24.99 once is a psychologically easy yes.
- **Beginner-friendly on purpose.** Plain-English "How it works" guide teaches the six core ideas from zero.
